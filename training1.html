Minimum Standards for Maintaining Student Membership

Zazen at SMZC 3 times/week specific to Daisan schedule; no 'either/or' days; on time; full duration
Daisan 1 time/week
Interview As directed by Yoshin Sensei
Fushinsamu 2 times/month
Sesshin 3 times/year (at least one full-time)
Zen Life Intensive 2 Prior to or next available after acceptance as a Student (ZLI 1 is prerequisiste)
Absences Limit of 3 non-communicated absences in a calendar year
Financial Integrity $60/month minimum SMZC, $20/month minimum The Hunger Project. Dues unpaid for more than 90 days voids membership.
Continuance At Yoshin Sensei's discretion
